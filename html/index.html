WHOMST?

Tommy Orion is a up-and-coming one man army: Producer. Singer. Songwriter. Instrumentalist. Engineer. He’s crafted his own music and visuals in his San Francisco apartment while going to Berkeley. He upcoming EP, “talking,” combines energetic and fun electronic production with unapologetically raw lyrics that tell stories of queer love and heartbreak. As his first major release, it showcases the wide range of sounds he commands as he works to establish his musical identity.

Transcending genre, Tommy creates a variety of sounds that can both live in the club and comfort someone on a quiet night. He draws inspiration from many different sources. His music channels the sound of pop mainstays like The Weeknd, Dua Lipa, Slayyyter, and Artemas. He also finds company in the alternative indie scene with artists like Julien Baker, Conan Gray, ELIO, and Caroline Polachek.
